## 📊 Agile Adoption Rates – Key Statistics (Global)

### **Overall adoption**

*   **71% of organizations use Agile in their software development lifecycle (SDLC)** [\[businesswire.com\]](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20240116199385/en/17th-State-of-Agile-Report-71-Use-Agile-in-their-SDLC-Small-Organizations-Report-Strong-Business-Benefits-Medium-and-Larger-Sized-Companies-Continue-to-Experience-Barriers-in-Successfully-Scaling-Agile), [\[pmwares.com\]](https://pmwares.com/summary-of-the-17th-annual-state-of-agile-report-summary-key-findings/)
*   **87% of organizations report practicing Agile in some form** (team-level or hybrid) [\[gitnux.org\]](https://gitnux.org/agile-statistics/)
*   **At least 75% of U.S. companies actively use Agile practices** [\[zippia.com\]](https://www.zippia.com/advice/agile-statistics/), [\[vact.com\]](https://www.vact.com/agile-adoption-statistics-2026/)

***

### **By company type & scale**

*   **95% of Fortune 500 companies use Agile for software development**, with many expanding it beyond IT [\[gitnux.org\]](https://gitnux.org/agile-statistics/)
*   Only **37% of organizations have adopted Agile across the entire enterprise**; most limit it to IT or engineering [\[gitnux.org\]](https://gitnux.org/agile-statistics/)
*   **63% of organizations use hybrid models** (Agile + Waterfall, often called “Wagile”) [\[gitnux.org\]](https://gitnux.org/agile-statistics/)

***

### **By team / function**

*   **86% of software development teams use Agile** [\[mosaicapp.com\]](https://www.mosaicapp.com/post/agile-project-management-statistics-adoption-rates)
*   **48% of engineering and R\&D teams** use Agile (fastest-growing group) [\[mosaicapp.com\]](https://www.mosaicapp.com/post/agile-project-management-statistics-adoption-rates), [\[businessmap.io\]](https://businessmap.io/blog/agile-statistics)
*   **28% of business operations teams** and **20% of marketing teams** have adopted Agile practices [\[pmwares.com\]](https://pmwares.com/summary-of-the-17th-annual-state-of-agile-report-summary-key-findings/)

***

### **By framework**

*   **Scrum:** \~87% of Agile teams [\[gitnux.org\]](https://gitnux.org/agile-statistics/), [\[mosaicapp.com\]](https://www.mosaicapp.com/post/agile-project-management-statistics-adoption-rates)
*   **Kanban:** \~56–62% (often used with Scrum) [\[gitnux.org\]](https://gitnux.org/agile-statistics/), [\[mosaicapp.com\]](https://www.mosaicapp.com/post/agile-project-management-statistics-adoption-rates)
*   **SAFe (Scaled Agile):** \~35–37% of organizations practicing Agile at scale [\[gitnux.org\]](https://gitnux.org/agile-statistics/)

***

### **Regional highlights**

*   **India:** \~77% Agile adoption among IT services companies [\[gitnux.org\]](https://gitnux.org/agile-statistics/)
*   **Europe:** \~58% adoption overall; Germany \~65% [\[gitnux.org\]](https://gitnux.org/agile-statistics/)
*   **UK:** \~64% of businesses have adopted Agile [\[gitnux.org\]](https://gitnux.org/agile-statistics/)

***

### **Strategic importance**

*   **91% of organizations say Agile adoption is a strategic priority** [\[parabol.co\]](https://www.parabol.co/resources/agile-statistics/)
*   **52% of organizations adopted Agile more than 5 years ago**, showing maturity rather than experimentation [\[gitnux.org\]](https://gitnux.org/agile-statistics/)
