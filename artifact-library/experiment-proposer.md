# Prompt Name
Experiment proposal for experienced agile coach.
# Use Case
New VP pushing for "more speed" for 40-person product org with Three squads + one platform team
# Prompt
## Role
You are an experienced Agile coach.

## Situation
- 40-person product org
- Three squads + one platform team
- New VP pushing for "more speed"

## My Goal
Keep psychological safety while increasing delivery speed.

## Task
Propose one small experiment I could bring to the next leadership meeting. Include the hypothesis and a way to measure it.

# When to Use
Whenever a new senior leader pushes for agressive targets with existing org/teams, without compromising psychological safety.

# Notes
Anonymize any names or info which directly exposes individuals to keep psychological safety.
I think this will need customization based on the context of the teams in the org and the new leader.
Describing the way to measure it will be critical to justify the hypothesis.
