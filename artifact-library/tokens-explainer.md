Tokens are the small pieces of text an AI reads and writes—roughly like words or parts of words.  
The more tokens you use, the more “thinking material” the AI has to process, which affects cost, speed, and how much context it can remember at once.

Using common OpenAI/GPT-style tokenization, **“scaling agile at a retail bank” is 6 tokens**.

(Exact counts can vary slightly by model/tokenizer, but for modern GPT models this phrase is typically tokenized as one token per word.)
